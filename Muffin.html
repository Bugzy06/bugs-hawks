Banana Chocolate Chip Muffins

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cup King Arthur Flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 3 large bananas
  • ¾ cup sugar
  • 1 egg
  • ⅓ cup butter, melted
  • ½ cup chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Line a muffin tin with cupcake liners. Preheat oven to 350°F
  2. Sift together King Arthur Flour, baking soda, and baking powder
  3. In a separate bowl, mix bananas, sugar, egg, and butter
  4. Pour the flour-baking soda-baking powder mix into the banana mixture. Mix well.
  5. Once mixed, add in the chocolate chips
  6. The consistency should be fairly liquidy, if not, add water 1 tablespoon at a time until the mixture isn’t as thick
  7. Pour the batter into a muffin tin
  8. Bake for 25-30 minutes
Total Time Makes
45 min 12 cupcakes
