Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

  • 1 pkg. (4 oz.) BAKER'S Unsweetened Chocolate
  • 1-3/4 cups sugar, divided
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1-2/3 cups flour
  • 1 tsp. baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp. salt
  • 1/2 cup butter or margarine, softened
  • 3 eggs
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1 tsp. vanilla

Directions

  1. Heat oven to 350Â°F
  2. Microwave chocolate, 1/2 cup sugar and water in large microwaveable bowl on HIGH 2 min. or until chocolate is almost melted, stirring after 1 min. Stir until chocolate is completely melted. Cool.
  3. Mix flour, baking soda and salt. Beat butter and remaining sugar in large bowl with mixer until light and fluffy. Add eggs, 1 at a time, beating after each until well blended. Beat in flour mixture alternately with milk. Add chocolate mixture and vanilla; mix well.
  4. Pour into 2 greased and floured 9-inch round pans.
  5. Bake 30 to 35 min. or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean. Cool in pan 10 min.; remove from pans to wire racks. Cool completely. Fill and frost with Easy Chocolate Frosting.
